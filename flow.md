The Problem We're Solving

Imagine a company gets hundreds of customer support emails every day — "I forgot my password," "My billing is wrong," "The website is down." Currently, a human has to read every single one, figure out what it's about, look up who the customer is, search through old solutions, and write a reply. This takes a lot of time, and most tickets are repetitive.

How Our AI Agent Fixes It

Step 1: Customer sends a support email
          ↓
Step 2: Our system receives it automatically (like a webhook from Zendesk)
          ↓
Step 3: AI Agent wakes up and asks:
          "What is this about?" → finds out it's a billing question
          "How urgent is this?" → marks it as medium priority
          ↓
Step 4: Agent looks up the customer in our database
          → Finds out they're a premium customer who pays $500/month
          ↓
Step 5: Agent searches our knowledge base for similar past solutions
          → Finds 3 relevant help articles about billing
          ↓
Step 6: Agent writes a draft reply using those articles
          → "Hi John, I see your invoice #1234 was charged twice..."
          ↓
Step 7: Draft reply goes to a HUMAN for approval
          → The support agent reviews it, maybe edits a line, clicks "Approve"
          ↓
Step 8: Reply is sent to the customer
